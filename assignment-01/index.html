Top 5 Travel Destinations

A list of my dream places to visit around the world.

  1. Tokyo, Japan

    A vibrant mix of traditional culture and futuristic innovation. I'd love to explore Shibuya Crossing and visit ancient temples.

  2. Paris, France

    Known for art, romance, and food. I want to see the Eiffel Tower and stroll through the Louvre.

  3. New York City, USA

    The city that never sleeps. From Times Square to Central Park, there's always something exciting happening.

  4. Barcelona, Spain

    Famous for its unique architecture by Gaudí and its beautiful Mediterranean beaches.

  5. Reykjavík, Iceland

    A gateway to natural wonders like the Northern Lights, glaciers, and geothermal hot springs.
